## Notes

1. **Login Required**: Facebook limits video visibility without login. To get the complete archive, you'd need to:
   - Log in to Facebook
   - Or use Facebook Graph API with appropriate permissions

2. **Date Format**: Videos use MMDDYY format in titles (e.g., "4826" = April 8, 2026)

3. **Meeting Types Identified**:
   - **PC** = Planning Commission
   - **HDC** = Historic District Commission
   - **Regular Session** = Mayor & Council regular meeting
   - **Work Session** = Mayor & Council work session
   - **Closed Session** = Executive session (usually brief)

4. **Parks Commission**: Not visible in public feed - may be posted less frequently or under different naming
